What do you need

WWe offer two different types of cover.

 

Covered for Life®

Your dog is insured for the rest of its life. You'll be covered for any new conditions as soon as you take out your policy. You can claim for conditions that become recurring as long as you renew each year.

OR

Time Limited

Your dog is insured for any new conditions for 12 months. If you claim for a condition in that time, you won't be covered for it in future years.
